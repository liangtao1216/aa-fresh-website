About AAFRESH Inc.

AAFRESH 是一家位於加利福尼亞州橙縣的現代化社區生鮮超市，致力於為當地居民提供高品質、每日更新的亞洲及美國家庭常用食品。我們結合線上 APP + 網站平台與線下門店，實現「當日下單、當日送達」的極速購物體驗。

AAFRESH is a modern community-based fresh grocery store located in Orange County, California. We offer high-quality, daily-updated Asian and American grocery essentials. By integrating our online APP + website with offline retail stores, we provide a seamless and fast "Same-Day Delivery" shopping experience.
